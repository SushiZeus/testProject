## 🎯 CALCULATIONS IMPLEMENTED

### PAYE (Tanzania 2026 Rates):
- 0 - 5,400,000: 0%
- 5,400,001 - 8,640,000: 8%
- 8,640,001 - 14,400,000: 20%
- 14,400,001 - 21,600,000: 25%
- 21,600,001+: 30%

### NSSF:
- Employee: 10% of gross pay
- Maximum: TZS 200,000/month

### Loan Deductions:
- Monthly = Loan Amount ÷ Repayment Months
- Automatic tracking
- Balance calculation
