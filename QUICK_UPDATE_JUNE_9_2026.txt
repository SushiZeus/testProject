    1. CLEARER PAYMENT BUTTONS
       ───────────────────────
       OLD: Generic "CONFIRM PAYMENT" button
       NEW: "PORT CHARGES PAID" (SEA) or "SWISSPORT PAID" (AIR)
       
       ✅ Finance knows exactly what they're confirming
       ✅ Blue buttons clearly labeled

    2. SIMPLIFIED WORKFLOW
       ────────────────────
       OLD: Payment → Cargo Cleared → Operations Done
       NEW: Payment → Operations Done → Verification Form
       
       ✅ Removed confusing "CARGO CLEARED" step
       ✅ Single GREEN "OPERATIONS DONE" button
       ✅ Same workflow for SEA and AIR

    3. VERIFICATION FORM ANYTIME
       ──────────────────────────
       OLD: Only after operations done
       NEW: Available for all shipments anytime
       
       ✅ Operation clerks can fill form proactively
       ✅ Optional (outline) when not required
       ✅ Required (purple pulsing) after operations done
       ✅ Always visible for viewing/printing

    FOR SEA SHIPMENTS:
    ─────────────────
    1. Operation Clerk uploads documents
    2. Finance clicks: [PORT CHARGES PAID] (Blue)
    3. Operation Clerk clicks: [OPERATIONS DONE] (Green)
    4. Operation Clerk fills: Verification Form (Purple)
    5. Ready for driver assignment

    FOR AIR SHIPMENTS:
    ─────────────────
    1. Operation Clerk uploads documents
    2. Finance clicks: [SWISSPORT PAID] (Blue)
    3. Operation Clerk clicks: [OPERATIONS DONE] (Green)
    4. Operation Clerk fills: Verification Form (Purple)
    5. Ready for driver assignment

            - View/Print Form
