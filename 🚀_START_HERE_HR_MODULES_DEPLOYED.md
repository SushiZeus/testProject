## 📈 CALCULATIONS

### PAYE (Tanzania 2026):
- 0 - 5.4M: 0%
- 5.4M - 8.64M: 8%
- 8.64M - 14.4M: 20%
- 14.4M - 21.6M: 25%
- 21.6M+: 30%

### NSSF:
- 10% of gross pay
- Maximum: TZS 200,000/month

### Loan Deductions:
- Monthly = Amount ÷ Months
- Automatic balance tracking
